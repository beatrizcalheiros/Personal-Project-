Hi! My name is Leonor Saunders! I'm a fine arts student from Portugal, part-time nerd and full-time lover of dogs. Currently,i'm studying painting in Porto's Fine Arts University. I'm not either the perfect sketcher or painter but...well...this is one of the many things i like to do. My work lays a lot in what, in my opinion, should be an artist's best supply: the sketchbook. I love going outdoors to sketch , wether it's a group of people in a coffee shop, or a landscape or.... anything else! Hope you enjoy it!
